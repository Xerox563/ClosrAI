🚀 ClosrAI - AI Sales Autopilot

ClosrAI is a 24/7 Virtual Sales Representative that automates prospecting, personalization, and outreach.

🛠 HOW TO TEST THE PLATFORM:

1. START THE BACKEND:
   - Go to /backend
   - Run: ./venv/bin/uvicorn main:app --reload --port 8001
   - Verify: Check http://localhost:8001/ - you should see "online" status.

2. START THE FRONTEND:
   - Go to /frontend
   - Run: npm run dev
   - Open: http://localhost:3000

3. THE TESTING FLOW:
   - SETTINGS: Go to Settings and save your "Sender Name" and "Calendly Link".
   - CAMPAIGNS: Create a new campaign with at least 2 steps (Initial + Follow-up).
   - LEAD DISCOVERY: Go to Search, find leads, select them, and add them to your campaign.
   - OUTREACH: Go to the Leads page, click a lead to open the AI Drawer.
   - AI GENERATION: Click "Generate Email" - Gemini AI will write a personalized pitch.
   - SENDING: Click "Send via Resend" to trigger the actual email delivery.

✨ CORE FEATURES:
- AI Lead Enrichment & Discovery
- Gemini 2.0 Personalized Email Generation
- Multi-step Automated Sequences
- Sentiment-based Auto-replies (The Closer)
- Real-time Outreach History Tracking
